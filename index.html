Apex Legends: Personal favorites

  1. Wraith
  2. Wattson
  3. Octane
  4. Pathfinder

Wraith

Out of all the characters there are to choose from, Wraith is by far my favorite. I find her wide array of abilities the most useful. Her abilities consist of void walking, placing a portal, and voices from the void. Void walking allows you to phase or become invisible for a couple seconds. Placing a portal allows you to place a portal for a set distance, allowing your teammates to also go through it to escape a bad situation. Voices from the void is a passive ability that notifies you when another player is aiming at you.

Wattson

If Wraith is taken by another player Wattson is my second go to. Shes a little but of a change of pace. While Wraith is an offensive character, Wattson is a defensive character. Her abilities consist of laying fences and the interception pylon. Her fences can create up to 6 electric walls at a time. These walls stun enemies and damage them if crossed. She can also place the interception pylon, which if you're near it charges your shields and blocks incoming bombs. So anything thrown or shot from the sky gets shot down.
