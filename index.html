Wraith

Out of all the characters there are to choose from, Wraith is by far my favorite. I find her wide array of abilities the most useful. Her abilities consist of void walking, placing a portal, and voices from the void. Void walking allows you to phase or become invisible for a couple seconds. Placing a portal allows you to place a portal for a set distance, allowing your teammates to also go through it to escape a bad situation. Voices from the void is a passive ability that notifies you when another player is aiming at you.
